---
title: 'Get Product'
api: 'GET /api/products/{id}'
description: 'Retrieve a single product by ID with related products'
---

Returns detailed information about a specific product along with related products based on shared brand or category.

## Path Parameters

<ParamField path="id" type="integer" required>
  The unique identifier of the product
</ParamField>

## Related Products Logic

Related products are determined by:
- Products sharing the same brand ID (ProductController.java:297)
- Products sharing at least one category ID (ProductController.java:298)
- Only active products are included (ProductController.java:295)
- The requested product itself is excluded from related products (ProductController.java:296)

## Error Cases

- **404 Not Found**: Product ID does not exist
- **404 Not Found**: Product exists but is not active (active=false)
